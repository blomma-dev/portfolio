About

Founded in 2023, Blomma Games is a small indie game development studio from Finland. Quite new to the scene, and we are dedicated to creating fun and engaging games for all ages.

Our first game, Melon Man is set to release on Steam in spring/Summer 2024.
We are excited to share our passion for gaming with the world and hope you enjoy our games as much as we do!
